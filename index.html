Improving Accessibility

Accessibility ensures that all users, including those using assistive technologies, can navigate and understand your content.

  • Use semantic HTML5 landmarks like <header>, <main>, <section>, and <footer>.
  • Provide descriptive alt attributes for images.
  • Ensure keyboard-friendly navigation and logical heading order.

Boosting SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) helps search engines index and rank your content appropriately.

  1. Use descriptive and unique titles and meta descriptions.
  2. Structure content with headings in proper hierarchy (H1, H2, etc.).
  3. Write meaningful link text (avoid “click here”).
